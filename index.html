Grading & phases

Moves are graded by centipawns lost versus the engine's best move; lower is better. A position is flagged when your move falls below the acceptable grade for its phase of the game.

Opening book (Engine mode)

Engine mode only: established theory (via the Lichess opening explorer) is exempt from flagging while "ignore book" is on.

My lines: moves to never flag

Paste a line in algebraic notation (move numbers optional). Every position→move in it is exempt from flagging — for offbeat lines you play on purpose. You can also tap "ignore" on any move in a position's history.

Repertoire courses

The courses your games are compared against in Repertoire mode. Courses crawled from Chessly ship with the app; add more by importing a crawl file or pasting lines.

Or paste lines (one per row, or a PGN). Both sides' moves become part of the course tree.
